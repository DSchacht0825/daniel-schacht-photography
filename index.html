The Professional Headshot Photography Process

My headshot photography sessions are designed to be comfortable, efficient, and results-driven. Here's what you can expect when you book a session with Daniel Schacht Photography:

Pre-Session Consultation

Before your session, we'll discuss your goals, industry, and how you plan to use your headshots. This ensures we create images that align with your brand and professional objectives. Whether you need corporate headshots for a law firm, creative shots for an acting portfolio, or approachable images for a therapist's website, we'll tailor the session to your needs.

Professional Studio Session

Sessions take place in my San Diego photography studio with professional lighting, multiple backdrops, and a relaxed atmosphere. I guide you through poses and expressions that feel natural while looking polished. Most clients are surprised at how comfortable and even fun the process is. Sessions typically last 30-60 minutes, with options for multiple outfit changes to give you variety.

Expert Retouching & Delivery

After your session, I carefully select the best images and provide professional retouching that maintains your natural appearance while ensuring you look your absolute best. You'll receive high-resolution digital files perfect for print, web, and social media use. Turnaround time is typically 3-5 business days, with rush delivery available if needed.
